This Privacy Policy applies to this website, our public communications, business correspondence, support interactions, and any mobile or online products, live services, or applications that link to or reference this Privacy Policy. Additional product-specific notices may apply where required by law or platform rules.

We may collect the following categories of information:

  • Technical information such as IP address, approximate location derived from IP, browser type, device type, operating system, language settings, referring URLs, and page usage events.
  • Transaction and service information where our products support in-app purchases, subscriptions, virtual item entitlements, or customer support actions.
  • Advertising and attribution information where our products use ad monetization, including ad engagement events, campaign identifiers, attribution signals, consent state, and anti-fraud information.

4. How We Use Information

We use information for the following purposes:

  • To operate, maintain, secure, and improve our website, products, and live services.
  • To respond to business, technical, and customer support inquiries.
  • To manage accounts, deliver purchased content or services, and prevent abuse or fraud.
  • To measure product performance, reliability, retention, and monetization outcomes.
  • To serve, limit, personalize, or measure advertising where legally permitted and supported by applicable consent signals.
  • To comply with legal, regulatory, contractual, platform, and policy obligations.
  • To establish, exercise, or defend legal claims and enforce our terms and policies.

Where laws such as the GDPR, UK GDPR, or similar regulations apply, we rely on one or more lawful bases including performance of a contract, compliance with legal obligations, legitimate interests, and consent where required. 7. Children & Age Restrictions

This website is not directed to children under the age of 13. Our products and services are also not intended for users below the minimum age required by applicable law to provide valid consent for data processing, behavioral advertising, or in-app purchases in their region, unless a legally valid parent or guardian authorization is obtained where such authorization is permitted.

If you are under 13, or under a higher local digital consent age such as 14, 15, or 16 where applicable, do not provide personal information through this website or our products without verified permission from a parent or legal guardian. If we learn that we have collected personal information from a child in violation of applicable law, we will take steps to delete the information or otherwise comply with legal requirements.

  • Professional advisers, auditors, regulators, law enforcement, or courts where required or legally justified.
